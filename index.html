Chu Shijian

The King of Tobacco and Oranges

After being released on medical parole in 2002, he started his second company at age 75, an orange plantation with the brand name "Chu Orange".

Here's a timeline of Mr.Chu's life:

  • 1928 - Born in Yunnan, China.
  • 1949 - Joined the army.
  • 1953(+) - Worked as a government servant.
  • 1955 - Get married with Ma Jingfen.
  • 1958 to 1978(-) - Worked in farms and finally became the CEO of sugar factories.
  • 1979(+) - Worked as the CEO of Yuxi Tobacco Company.
  • 1995(-) - Was accused of corruption and his daughter committed suicide.
  • 1996 to 2001(-) - Was in prison and sick.
  • 2002 - Bailed out and started to plant orange trees.
  • 2012(+) - His orange brand became famous and successful.
  • 2019 - Died at age of 91

“回想这么多年来，我自己做得最问心无愧的就是：没有庸庸碌碌地生活。我十几岁在家乡时就帮着母亲谋生，从那时起，我就没有闲下来过，更没有混过日子。几十年来，我扛过枪打过仗，也曾经在政府机关任职，后来则是长期做企业经营的事情；曾经有过人人都羡慕的辉煌，也跌落到人生最低谷过。不管在什么阶段，在什么年龄，我都在全心全意地做事，一个人不虚度时光，要对国家对社会有贡献，人生才有价值。”
